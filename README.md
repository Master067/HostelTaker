# HostelTaker
Hostel Taker is an Android application that is helpful for any hostel_taker/Wardens/Managers who
want to maintain their hostel digitally.<br>
In the current scenario, wardens have to maintain lots of registers for storing student's personal
information, their daily attendance, their payment details, etc.<br><br>
But what if they maintain all this information on their mobile App.<br><br>
So Hostel Taker will fulfill their need, in addition to that, it has many features such as:-<br><br>
1. Wardens can maintain details of Hostellars ( Along with their ID proof ).
2. one can check room availability.
3. Wardens can take daily Attendance.
4. Hostellers and their Parents can also check their attendance, also they can edit their personal info.
5. Wardens can also maintain payment related details of Hostellers.
6. Wardens can also make announcements on it.
7. Parents and wardens can also communicate with each other with the help of this (through
messaging).
